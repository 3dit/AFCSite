Our Kitchen

Our Mission

At Allergen Free Cooking, we believe that food allergies should never stand between you and delicious, satisfying meals. Founded in 2020, our mission is to provide trusted, thoroughly tested recipes and practical guidance for families navigating the challenges of cooking with food allergies.

What We Do

Every recipe on our site is developed and tested multiple times in our kitchen. We work with registered dietitians and allergists to ensure our content is both accurate and helpful. Our recipes cover the full range of common allergens, and each one is clearly labeled so you always know exactly what's safe for your family.

Our Promise

We take food allergies seriously. Every recipe is tested for safety and taste. We never use misleading labels, and we always clearly note when a recipe may not be suitable for severe allergies. Your family's safety is our top priority.
